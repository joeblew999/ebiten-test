# YouTube 

## YouTube Accepted Video Formats

YouTube accepts the following video file formats: .mp4, .mpv, .mpeg, .wmv, .avi, .mpegps, .flv, 3GPP, and WebM.

### Complete Format List:
- **MP4** (.mp4)
- **MOV** (.mov)
- **MPEG** (.mpeg, .mpv)
- **AVI** (.avi)
- **WMV** (.wmv)
- **MPEG-PS** (.mpegps)
- **FLV** (.flv)
- **3GPP** (3GPP)
- **WebM** (.webM)
- **HEVC/H.265** (supported)

## Recommended Best Format

MP4 is the best video format for YouTube with AAC-LC audio codec and H.264 video codec, and a standard aspect ratio of 16:9. YouTube recommends the use of MP4 and if you upload other less-used formats, the platform converts them into MP4.

## Important Specifications (2024-2025):

**File Size & Length:**
- Maximum file size: 128GB
- Maximum video length: 12 hours

**Aspect Ratio:**
- The most versatile video aspect ratio, which is also recommended by YouTube, is 16:9
- For YouTube Shorts: 9:16

**Note:** YouTube does not support uploading videos in MKV format directly, though WebM which is based on Matroska is compatible and is accepted by YouTube.
